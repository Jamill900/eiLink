About

In 2015, aiming to develop research capability in education Khazar University and a group of professionals from Oil and Gas Industry collaboratively established eiLink R&D Center (which stands for Education to Industry Link). The initial idea behind this center was to create the mutual opportunity for Education and Industry to benefit from each other through collaborative research and scientific studies.

Today eiLink is much more than research and development centers, it is a business unit that provides value-added services addressing customer's emerging technical and technological challenges. Here ideas, studies and developments turn into brand-new R&D products and Innovative Solutions that have direct and tangible link to the industry and business.

Our Vision

To be the partner of choice R&D Center in the country.

Activities at eiLink R&D Center are focused on high quality researchers which is fundamental gor delivery of sophisticated technical solutions, services, and products driven by applicable, publishable, and commercially viable results. Their eager desire for more nuanced understanding of fundamental and applied science is a powerful motivation for research. But pursuit of commercial success is the compelling driver. eiLink R&D Center aims to create an environment for these forces to interact and reinforce one another to support the paradigm shift of R&D in the country.
