Research Areas

Leveraging multi-domain knowledge, eiLink pursues technical excellence and brings effective solutions in the following areas:

Geology and Geophysics

Results are more precise when the subsurface is better understood. In addition to assisting customers with a clear understanding of the subsurface, eiLink also aims to help them make the necessary adjustments to new information.

Oil & Gas Production Technology

Whether it is production optimization or tackling challenges with carbonate production, eiLink helps customers using cutting edge or more orthodox solutions.

Drilling & Well Integrity

Leveraging extensive subsurface knowledge, eiLink provides solutions to customers regardless of the complexity of the challenges they are facing.

Computational Fluid Dynamics

eiLink believes Digitar Rocks is a step toward further industrial digitalization. Using Digital Rocks for mesoscopic fluid simulations and improved subsurface characterization, a precise picture of subsurface behavior could be made.

Thermodynamic Modelling

eiLink works to provide customers with information beyond simple mass flow by using fluid simulations. Our objective is to offer solutions based on accurate physical models using flow simulations and sophisticated thermodynamic models.

Reservoir Modelling

Whether it is digital reservoir characterization or macro-scale fluid simulations, eiLink helps customers to get a better grasp on their assets, as well as how to utilize them in the most optimal way.

Software Engineering

Complex theories are unhelpful without effective application. eiLink strives to assist its customers in streamlining their workflows as much as possible, whether it be by integrating cutting-edge solutions into current workflows or by developing specialized software to meet their needs.

Data Science

Data Science is being used by eiLink in conjunction with large amounts of customer data in an effort to solve complex problems that go beyond a simple physical understanding of the procedure.

Machine Learning

eiLink is committed to keeping on the front edge of technology. eiLink meets customers' needs in the best way possible by utilizing AI to assist our customers or Deep Learning to find potential connections in data.
